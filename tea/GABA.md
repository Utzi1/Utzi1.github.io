# What's GABA?

![GABA](gaba.png)

Gamma-aminobutyric acid, γ-aminobutyric acid is an inhibitory neurotransmitter that is produced by decarboxylation of glutamate with the help of glutamate decarboxylase (GAD).
The isoforms GAD65 and GAD67 are predominantly active to maintain the concentration of GABA in humans.
Studies suggest, that the intak of GABA stimulates concentration and acts in a calming manner. [^2][^1]

# What's GABA?

![GABA](gaba.png)

Gamma-aminobutyric acid, γ-aminobutyric acid is an inhibitory neurotransmitter that is produced by decarboxylation of glutamate with the help of glutamate decarboxylase (GAD).[^1]
The isoforms GAD65 and GAD67 are predominantly active to maintain the concentration of GABA in humans.[^2]

[^1]: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/36988214/
[^2]: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40427402/

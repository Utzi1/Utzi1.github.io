# Orange Vermouth

No clue what orange wine is, however, big clue what orange vermouth could b3!
In fact maybe it was invented by me.
To make it, macerate 3 sprigs of Artemisia absinthium in 1L of medium sweet meet (honey wine) for 2 weeks.
Do the same with 3 sprigs of Artemisia absinthium and 1L of dry white wine, alternatively you can use 1L of white "Glühwein" (mulled wine) of very high quality.
After 2 weeks, strain the liquid and mix the two together.
Add 50gh of oleo saccarum of orange and lemon.
Further add 100ml of elderflower syrup.
Adjust the acidity with a 10% solution of 50/50 citric and tartaric acid, to taste.
The flavor should be balanced between sweet, bitter and acidic, with a strong herbal note.

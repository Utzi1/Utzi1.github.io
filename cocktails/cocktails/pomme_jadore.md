A very simple, equal parts cocktail, with one exception

  - 1 part Tequila (Reposado)
  - 1 part Calvados
  - 1 part Maraschino
  - 1 part Lime juice
  - 1 cl Ferro China Bisleri

The Ferro China Bisleri adds a very, almost crisp, note that harmonizes with the Maraschino and creates, in combination with the Calvados
a almost apple-like flavor.
The addition of malic acid instead of lime juice may be worth trying.

Shake well over cold ice and strain into a chilled coupette glass.

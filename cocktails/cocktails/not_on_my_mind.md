# Not-what-was-on-my-mind

It really wasn't, i grabbed the wrong bottle in the store.
A strange mistake as the mission was to buy Arrak and not Arak.
Here a single letter makes the difference between a spirit made from sugarcane and one made from grapes and aniseed.
Well, I grabbed the latter.
My home bar is well stocked and there are a few aniseed derivatives, and another one was not strictly necessary.
This is a Monkey-Gland-Not-On-My-Mind, a cocktail that is not on my mind, but now that It was made, the world needs to know:

  - 1 & 3/4 oz Rhum Agricole (Habitation saint-Etienne Blanc
  - 1 oz Arak
  - 1 oz Orange Juice
  - 3/4 oz Lemon Juice
  - 1 tsp Honey

Smear the honey on the inside (as much as possible, that will help dissolving it) of a shaker and combine all ingredients, try to incorporate the honey as much as possible, then add ice and shake hard.
Strain into a chilled cocktail glass and garnish with an orange twist.
